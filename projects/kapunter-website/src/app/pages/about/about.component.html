Who we are

Kapunter is a sports-gaming account and wallet management platform. We do not operate as a game board ourselves. Instead, we help players create and manage IDs on external gaming sites, move funds securely between their Kapunter wallet and those sites, and track every transaction with complete clarity.

Since {{ brand.foundedYear }}, our focus has remained unchanged: make deposits instant, keep withdrawals predictable, and put real human support within a WhatsApp message's reach — morning, noon or midnight.

Today, Kapunter combines a polished web experience with an official Android APK, a bonus & lossback program, referral rewards, and an admin-grade operations backbone that keeps the platform running smoothly for thousands of users.
